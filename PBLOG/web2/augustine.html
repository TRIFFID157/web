About St. Augustine

A Saint and a philosopher of the church. He follows the concept that God embraces us all, he said that everything will be better if we are with God. For him God and his teachings affect several aspects in life. He relates our existence to God being modeled in his likeness though being alive means that we are still far From God and has yet to be true with him.
